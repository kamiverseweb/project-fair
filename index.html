(4)  DO I NEED TRAVEL INSURANCE?

You’ll need money while traveling, so pack your credit
or debit cards for withdrawing foreign cash from ATMs.
Skip transferring money at the airport and do it
at a bank in your destination; you’ll get more
value for your money as airports often charge high commission rates.
Consider getting a card that doesn’t charge foreign
transaction fees so you can save more money while travelling.
